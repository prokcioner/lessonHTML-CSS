What is a CMS

CMS - "content managment system"(engine) - is the basis for a website written by PHP programmers,with the help of which you can manage the site (add content, change menu items, etc.) without knowing HTML and CSS.

However, in order to make a website with the help of a CMS, you will need services of a programmer, a designer, a layout designer and investments.

What kind of CMS are there

There are various content managment systems: for online stores, for blogs, for forums, etc.

CMS examples

Examples of popular CMS: Joomla, WordPress(for blogs), PhpBB(for forums).

CMS are paid and free
